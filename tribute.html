Elon Musk

The Visionary Engineer

    A look at the amazing life of Elon Musk:

  • 1971 - Born in Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa to Engineer father and Model mother
  • 1982 - Sold first computer program for $500
  • 1989 - Moved to Canada to attend Queen's University, avoided mandatory service in South AfriCan Military
  • 1992 - Left to study Business and Physics from University of Pennysylvania
  • 1994 - Gets Wharton Economics degree and later Physics degree
  • 1995 - Starts Zip2 with brother
  • 1999 - Gets $22 million from sale of Zip2 and starts X.com
  • 2000 - Merges X.com with Confinity forming PayPal, Gets Married to girlfriend Justine and gets ousted as CEO from PayPal while on honeymoon
  • 2002 - Started SpaceX
  • 2004 - Invested in Tesla
  • 2006 - Invested in SolarCity
  • 2008 - Divorced Justine Musk, Won NASA contract worth $1.6B
  • 2009 - Unveiled Model S
  • 2010 - Married Talulah Riley
  • 2011 - Announced reusable rockets and promised to send humans to Mars
  • 2012 - Unveiled Model X and Divorced Riley
  • 2013 - Married Riley again, published the design of Hyperloop
  • 2017 - Started The Boring Company, Divorced Riley again
